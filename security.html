24/7 Security Monitoring

Professional-grade cameras and NVR recording protect your belongings around the clock at both locations.

Our System

Professional Series Cameras

We deploy commercial-grade PoE cameras across both of our Hutchinson and South Hutchinson facilities. These cameras deliver ultra-high-definition video, AI-powered smart detection, and full-color night vision—all recorded to a centralized NVR so footage is always available when needed.

Whether you're storing furniture during a renovation or keeping seasonal equipment safe year-round, our camera system provides an extra layer of confidence that your belongings are protected.

Camera Capabilities

What Our Cameras Can Do

Every camera on our property is equipped with these professional-grade features.

4K+ Ultra HD

Up to 16MP resolution captures sharp detail at every angle, day or night.

AI-Powered Detection

Powered by ReoNeura AI—smart alerts for people, vehicles, and animals while filtering out false alarms from wind or shadows.

Color Night Vision

ColorX technology delivers full-color footage even in low-light conditions—not just black and white.

Built for Kansas Weather

Rated for −22°F to 131°F with metal housings that withstand rain, snow, and summer heat.

Vandal-Resistant Design

Reinforced dome and turret housings protect the cameras themselves from tampering.

Remote Live Viewing

Our team can monitor live feeds and review recordings remotely from any device, any time.

Facility Features
